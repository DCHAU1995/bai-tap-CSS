5.Groups of selectors

A comma-separated list of selectors represents the union of all elements selected by each of the individual selectors in the list. (A comma is U+022C.)For example, in CSS when several selectors share the same declarations, they may be grouped into a comma-separated list. White space may appear and/or after the comma.
